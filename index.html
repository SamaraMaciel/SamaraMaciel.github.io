This is the title page for Group 2's HTML assignment. The group members are Janelle, Laarni, Samara and Sydney.

As a group we decided to make the "theme" of this site food related, with the conclusion to share some recipes. On each members page is a recipe, with ingredients, measurement and instructions on how to make each dish/dessert. Each page is unique just like the recipes it contains.

At the top you will find the links to each of our pages, and at the bottom we have some fun!
